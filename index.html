Mancala

How to play

  1. Each player has a store at one end of the board. Player 1 on the right, Player 2 on the left.
  2. Player 1 starts by clicking one of the six boxes on the bottom row. Stones move counter-clockwise, depositing one into each subsequent box until stones run out. Player 2 starts by clicking a box in the top row.
  3. If a player drops a stone in their own store, they score a point. Players may not score for the opponent.
  4. If the last stone is dropped in the player's store, they get another turn.
  5. If the last stone is dropped in an empty box on the player's own side, they get that stone and any stones in the box directly opposite.
  6. The game ends when one row of six boxes is completely empty. The player with stones remaining in their row captures all remaining stones. The player with the most stones in their store wins.
